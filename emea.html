WHAT DOES THE 2021 VALORANT CHAMPIONS TOUR LOOK LIKE FOR THE YEAR?

The VCT is broken down into 3 stages throughout the year that include Challengers and Masters. Stage 1 played in February and March; Stage 2 played in May and June; and Stage 3 from July to September. In October, there is a Last Chance Qualifier and finally, we end the year with a world championship, the VCT Champions, where the best of the best will fight for the chance to become the first world champions!

WHAT DOES EMEA REGION MEAN?

The EMEA region is the name for the competitive region that encompasses: Europe, Turkey, Russia, CIS and MENA.
